Bio

I grew up in a small town called Walden, NY. It is 1 hour and 45 minutes South of Siena. I love playing and watching basketball during my free time. I attended Valley Central High School where I played on the basketball team for four years. I still play here at Siena; I play pick-up and intramurals at the MAC. My first job was a Pizza Delivery Driver for Guido's Pizza in Walden which I still do from time to time. I like my job because I get to drive my car. I love driving around, listening to music, in the summer time. Each year my family and I take a vacation to Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. It is one of our family traditions.

Goals

My professional goal is to get into the Cyber Security field. The topic of Information Security is very interesting to me. My short term goal is to first graduate college with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Criminal Justice. Once I have an established career in the Cyber Security field, I want to travel to different countries. I have not had the opportunity to go abroad yet in my life and I want to see other countries and cultures. I feel that would be a fun experience. I want to go to an English Premier League game and see some of the famous soccer stadiums in Europe.
