Primitive Sponge

Primitive Sponge, also incorrectly known as SpongeGar, is a meme that originated on 4chan in 2014 and gained popularity in 2016. The meme depicts Primitive Sponge from the 1999 SpongeBob SquarePants episode "SB-129" in an alarmed pose.
